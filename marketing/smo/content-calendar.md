## UTM Parameters

All links should include:
- `utm_source=[platform]` (instagram, pinterest, etc.)
- `utm_medium=social`
- `utm_campaign=[campaign_name]`
- `utm_content=[post_type]`

Example: `?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=project_showcase&utm_content=dubai_palace`
